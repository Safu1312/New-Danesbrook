What Families Say

The trust families place in us is our greatest honor. Here are their authentic experiences with our care approach.

Julie B
Daughter of Resident
"My Mum started at Danesbrook House on respite, and has been a permanent resident for two weeks. I had done my research beforehand, and Danesbrook has lived up to my expectations. The staff and management are kind, caring and approachable, and I have seen how they treat the residents as individuals, according to their different needs and personalities. Danesbrook provides a wide variety of activities, so there is something for everyone, and residents can join in, or not, as they choose. Coupled with the social interaction with other residents and staff, this has meant that my Mam has settled in quicker than I could have hoped for. Danesbrook is clean and well maintained, with a lovely, welcoming atmosphere. They offer a varied menu throughout the week, with lots of tea, coffee and biscuits. The welcome also extends to visitors. I live away, but have the peace of mind of knowing that my Mam is safe and happy, as that is exactly what I see when I visit each week."
Resident since January 2023
Gareth G
Son of Resident
"Mum has been at Danesbrook for about a year. We had checked out quite a few care homes in and around Cardiff, but the moment we walked into Danesbrook we were struck by what a loving and caring atmosphere the place has. This is clearly down to the staff and management, who all work so brilliantly as a team to make everyone feel as safe and cared-for as possible. The residents are well fed and catered for, with plenty of activities and visiting guests to keep them occupied during the week, and the rooms are comfortable and cleaned daily. We also thoroughly appreciate the central garden area, which is well-designed and maintained. When it came to deciding where to place Mum, the choice was easy, as we are totally confident that she is as safe as possible, and really well cared for."
Resident since September 2024

Daily Life & Activities

Music & Movement

Sing-along sessions, visiting performers and gentle chair-based exercise classes.

Arts & Crafts

Seasonal projects, pottery, knitting clubs and gallery visits for creative expression.

Outings & Community

Weekly minibus trips to local markets, seaside walks, and inter-care-home bowling leagues.

Spaces that Feel Like Home

Choose from cosy private rooms, quiet reading corners, or lively communal lounges. Each space is designed for comfort, safety and independence.

  • Private en-suite bedrooms (TV & Wi-Fi included)
  • Two stylish lounges overlooking the gardens
  • Hairdressing salon & therapy suite
  • 24-hour call system and secure access

Food & Nutrition
